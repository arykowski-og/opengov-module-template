## Defect Management

### Defect Lifecycle
1. **Discovery** - Identify the issue
2. **Classification** - Assign severity/priority
3. **Analysis** - Root cause investigation
4. **Tracking** - Monitor resolution
5. **Verification** - Confirm fix
6. **Regression** - Ensure no side effects

### Severity Levels
| Severity | Description | SLA |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| Critical | System down, no workaround | 4 hours |
| High | Major feature broken | 1 day |
| Medium | Feature partially working | 3 days |
| Low | Minor issue, workaround exists | 1 week |
